Software
The Infobahn utilizes teleconferencing technology to provide a digital space in which to conduct participatory online programming. In order to join The Infobahn, please visit zoom.us to learn more about installing and setting up Zoom.

Scheduling
Scheduled programming is announced sporadically, and a full calendar of planned events can be accessed here.

Privacy
All sessions of The Infobahn are recorded and uploaded to the archive on the site. If you do not wish to be recorded, you may participate without your microphone or camera.

Participation
Visitors of The Infobahn are expected to show respect and courteousness for other visitors. Visitors who are disruptive and disrespectful will be immediately removed.
