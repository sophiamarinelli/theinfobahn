About

The Infobahn is a public space that exists online as a website. It is free and utilizes teleconferencing technology to facilitate digital community and self discovery through free classes, group activities, lectures, and exhibitions.

The activites at The Infobahn provide opportunities to engage with and learn from other fellow visitors, known and unknown.
